# 27\_digitalization\_of\_the\_color\_attribute

Color represents the color grade of the gemstone. Higher grades are generated with lower probability.

| Color Grade | Probability | Score |
| ----------- | ----------- | ----- |
| D           | 1%          | 15    |
| E           | 2%          | 13    |
| F           | 4%          | 11    |
| G           | 8%          | 8     |
| H           | 15%         | 5     |
| I           | 20%         | 3     |
| J           | 25%         | 1     |
| K           | 25%         | 1     |
